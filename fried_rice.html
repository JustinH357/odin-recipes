Fried Rice

Description

Quick and easy fried rice recipe. Simples ingredients such as rice, eggs, peas, and soy sauce is all you need! This recipe can be made for large servings. This reciple is easily customizable with any other ingridents you feel like adding.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic or minced (optional)

Steps

  1. Assemble ingridents
  2. Place carrots in saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
