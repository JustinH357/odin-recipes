Omelet Rice

Description

A fantastic dish that can be serve during breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white or brown rice of your choice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoon ketchup
  • 2 thin slices ham cubed size
  • 1 slice processed cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Heat frying pan over medium heat coating with cooking spray.
  2. Add cooked rice, 2 tablespoon ketchup, ham, and cheese if needed. Cook and stir until ingredients are well combined and heated for about 8 minutes. Put the mixture onto a serving bowl and shape into a oval.
  3. Beat eggs, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Heat a small frying pan coated with cooking spray over heat.
  4. Add the bowl with eggs mixture on the small frying pan over medium heat. As eggs set, lift the edges to let uncooked portion flow underneath.
  5. Use a spatula to gently fold the eggs into a cocoon shape. When eggs are completely set, remove from the frying pan.
  6. Place omelet on top of rice and run a knife length-wise through the top layer of the omelet. Add 1 tablespoon of ketchup and sprinke of parsley to top.
